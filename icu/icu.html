About Our ICU Department

The Intensive Care Unit (ICU) at Berhampore Women and Child Care is designed to provide specialized care for critically ill patients. Our ICU is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and staffed by highly trained medical professionals who are dedicated to delivering round-the-clock care.

Features of Our ICU

Advanced life-support systems and monitoring equipment.

Expert team of doctors, nurses, and respiratory therapists.

Dedicated neonatal and pediatric intensive care units.

Strict infection control protocols to ensure patient safety.

Availability of diagnostic and imaging services.

We understand the emotional challenges of having a loved one in critical care. Our team is here to provide support and regular updates to families, ensuring clear communication and compassionate care.

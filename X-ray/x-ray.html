About Our X-Ray Department

The X-Ray department at Berhampore Women and Child Care is equipped with advanced imaging technology to provide accurate diagnostic services. Our highly skilled radiologists and technicians ensure that patients receive prompt and reliable results in a safe and comfortable environment.

Features of Our X-Ray Services

Digital X-Ray systems for high-quality imaging.

Fast and efficient image processing for timely diagnosis.

Specialized pediatric X-Ray facilities.

Stringent radiation safety protocols to protect patients and staff.

Collaborative approach with physicians for comprehensive care.

Our team is committed to providing exceptional care, ensuring that every patient’s diagnostic needs are met with precision and professionalism. Whether it’s routine imaging or emergency diagnostics, we are here to assist you.
